Yoga

Like for most of us, the pandemic was a very difficult time. My already haphazard exercise routine became non-existant. I'd tried yoga before. I once took an in-person class at a hostel in New Zealand and had tried a few online classes on YouTube. But my practice only took off once I'd found the right teacher for me. Yoga with Kassandra is my favourite channel and the one I practice with the most.

My favourite prop is a set of blocks I bought myself for my 36. birthday. Love them! The best part about Yoga: You can take classes to build up strength, get more flexible or just relax.

I'm up to 90min of exercise six days a week. Not always Yoga. But once you get your body moving, it tells you how to keep going.

I practiced:

  • breathing with movements
  • being present in my body
  • meditation
  • the joy of exercise
  • letting go

I learned:

  • how to relax or tense up specific muscle groups
  • exercise is fun and once it's a habit it becomes a must for a healthy body and mind
  • a little bit of yoga goes a long way. I started with ten minute practices a day!
  • that there are different kinds of yoga. I like vinyasa the most.
  • setting intentions for the day helps you centre and focus me
