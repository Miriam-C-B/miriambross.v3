Radio Show Host

In the winter semester in 2012 I was the producer of a talk show on University Radio Falmer. Producers were a new category at the student radio club and the the talk show I was now supposed to produce had run without a producer before so that was interesting. At the beginning of 2013 I decided to host my own radio show.

Every Saturday morning I would play a selection of the top ten songs of another country. Since I had a lot of international friends, I also soon found guests from the respective countries to interview. It was a lot of fun! I cannot remember what I called my show, sorry!

I practiced:

  • researching music
  • interviewing people
  • intercultural communication

I learned:

  • how to host a radio show
  • that it's a bad idea to make structural changes to an already existing and functioning team without explaining to them why the changes are necessary
  • always (!!!) check the quality of your output! Like, you know, the volume of your mics...
