Watch every Keanu Reeves movie

It all started with "Point Break". I'm a tad embarrassed to admit that it took me over thirty years to discover the movie and when I did, my first thought was "Where have you been all my life?" My second thought was "I wonder what else Keanu Reeves has been up to!" So I copied the filmography from Wikipedia (cause who doesn't love a list!) and worked my way through it. It's an ongoing project because some of the movies from the eighties and nineties are difficult to get a hold of. Also, the man has made A LOT of movies!

So far, I've watched 62 out of 73 and counting.

I practiced:

  • lots of sitting.
  • analysing movies, story structure and characterisations.
  • how to figure out exactly why I like or dislike a movie.

I learned:

  • Keanu Reeves has made a lot of movies.
  • the eighties were WILD!
  • the nineties were a lot weirder than I remembered.
  • what kind of creative I want to be: show up, do your best and ship, ship, ship!
