Knitting myself a scarf!

I am a very short person and the average scarf sold in shops is almost always way to long for comfort. So I thought I'd learn to knit and make my own scarf! The result you see below: two beautiful scarfs in the perfect length and knitted from the perfect material. One everyday one and a slightly fluffier version for special occasions.

I got also knitted a blanket the cat enjoyes sleeping on. The hat I made for myself was unwearable but it taught me a lot about the importance of the right yarn for a specific project.

Conclusion: In future, I'll knit my scarfs myself. The length is just too perfect to go back to shop-bought.

I practiced:

  • knitting
  • looking for useful videos on YouTube

I learned:

  • how to knit a scarf
  • how to knit a hat
  • that I don't enjoy knitting a hat or a blanket
  • different projects require different types of yarn to be successful
  • I'm somewhat compulsive about finishing projects so that knitting a blanket while watching TV was anything but relaxing.
